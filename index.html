STP showcasing is a three-venture way to deal with building a focus on promoting plans. The "S"refers to ‘Segmenting’, the "T" for ‘Targeting’ & the "P" stands for ‘Positioning’.

SEGMENTING

Market segmentation is a marketing concept that refers to arranging prospective consumers into groups or segments with comparable needs and responses to marketing activity. Market segmentation enables firms to reach out to different sorts of customers who place varied values on goods and services.. Market segmentation tries to reduce risk by assessing which items have the best probability of capturing a share of a target market and selecting the most effective delivery method for those products. This allows the organization to increase overall efficiency by focusing limited resources on activities that provide the best return on investment.
